Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 Jan 2026

1. Introduction

Sub Manage Hub (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is an internal, admin-only mobile application designed to help businesses record, manage, and monitor subscription sales and users.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What data we collect
  • Why we collect it
  • How the data is used and protected
  • Whether the app requires any device permissions

By using this application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Who Can Use This App

  • This application is not a public consumer app
  • It is intended only for authorized admin users
  • Accounts are created and managed externally by the app owner
  • There is no public sign-up inside the app

3. Purpose of the Application

The app helps administrators to:

  • Record subscription sales (monthly or yearly)
  • Track active and expired subscriptions
  • Search users by email or phone number
  • View sales activity and purchase history
  • Soft-delete and restore subscription records
  • Manage additional notification email addresses
  • Export business data (users, purchases, emails) via email

4. Information We Collect

The app collects only business-related data entered by authorized admins.

A. Admin Account Information

Collected via Firebase Authentication:

  • Admin email address
  • Authentication UID

Used for:

  • Secure login
  • User authorization
  • Export request validation

B. Subscription & User Data (Entered by Admin)

When an admin adds a record, the app may store:

  • Customer email address
  • Customer phone number (with country code)
  • Subscription plan (Monthly / Yearly)
  • Amount paid
  • Purchase date and expiry date

This information is entered manually by the admin and is used strictly for subscription management purposes.

C. Extra Notification Emails

Admins may optionally store additional email addresses used for reports or exports.

D. Export Metadata

When an export is requested, we store:

  • Requested email address
  • Export type
  • Export status and timestamps (queued, running, emailed, failed)

5. How We Use the Data

Collected data is used only to:

  • Display subscription records inside the app
  • Calculate active and expired subscriptions
  • Enable search and filtering
  • Send requested exports via email
  • Support restoration of recently deleted records
We do not sell, rent, share, or use data for advertising or marketing.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely using Firebase services
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication
  • App data is stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Export files are generated via secure Cloud Functions
  • Export files are sent as email attachments and are not publicly accessible

Soft-deleted records are:

  • Hidden from main screens
  • Retained for up to 30 days
  • Permanently deleted after the retention period

7. App Permissions

❌ No Special Device Permissions Required

This app does not request access to:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • SMS
  • Storage (photos or files)
  • Call logs

✅ Internet Access Only

  • Required to connect to Firebase services
  • Used for authentication, data synchronization, and exports

8. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions)
  • Email service (SendGrid) for delivering export files

These services process data only to provide core application functionality.

9. Data Retention

  • Active data is retained until deleted by an admin
  • Soft-deleted records are retained for 30 days
  • Deleted records are permanently removed after 30 days
  • Export records are retained only for audit and delivery tracking

10. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children and does not target users under the age of 13.

11. User Control & Responsibility

  • Admins are responsible for the data they enter
  • Admins should ensure they have legal rights to store customer information
  • Admins can delete or restore records within the app

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated “Last updated” date.
